Wekka

Experience builder

We have not launched our campaign yet - more information coming soon...

The traditional format of education, heavy on theory, is broken. Theory is not enough, practical experience is critical.
Not only for students, but for anyone interested in practical learning.
Let's complement the traditional (theory based) format of learning, with a platform designed for building hands-on experience.

This project is meant to involve all those who would benefit from helping learning: students or any individuals, companies and universities. Their involvement will provide:

  • students or individuals with opportunities for real, practical experience as they will work on actual existing company projects
  • companies with unlimited numbers of solutions, ideas, or future employees
  • professors with unlimited volume of real life cases and projects that can complete the classroom learning experience

    • Anyone can start a project

    Companies can outsource tasks, discover new ideas and solutions, find proven talent in a certain field, or share their interests.
    Individuals will practice their knowledge within a specific field or industry, while gaining experience and building a portfolio or network in a specific industry. Those who complete a project will receive a certificate.

    Universities and professors can use Wekka as practical supplement to their courses, to provide extra motivation or as a means to introduce competition among students. Projects can be part of the assessment scheme, extra-credit or can even replace short internships.
