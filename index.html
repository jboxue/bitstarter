Wekka.com

The experience builder

The traditional format of education, heavy on theory, is broken. Theory is not enough, practical experience is critical.
Not only for students, but for anyone who wants to learn something new, the practical way.
Let's complement the traditional (theory based) format of learning, with a platform designed for building hands-on experience.

This project is meant to involve all those who would benefit from helping learning: students, companies and universities. Their involvement will provide:

  • students with opportunities for real, practical experience as they will work on actual existing company projects
  • professors with an unlimited volume of real cases and projects that can offer a better learning experience to students
  • companies with valuable interns that can solve tasks of any difficulty level in any field of activity

    • Anyone can start a project

    Companies will launch projects as they need various tasks to be done; they may want to vet something, or to find new ideas, or to find proven talent in a certain field. Some companies may want to send their employees here, as a way to improve their practical knowledge of various fields/tasks, to rank employees on a specific skill, as competition, as a motivational tool, as a benchmark etc. This platform can also be used for marketing purposes.
    Individuals will join and complete projects because they either want to practice their knowledge within a specific field or industry, or they want to create or expand their experience, portfolio or network. They may do it for the credentials, or for the challenge.

    Universities will launch projects, and will direct their students here in their efforts to provide hands on experience for their students, to improve the employability of their students or additional s support for their courses.
    Professors will direct students here as practical supplement to theoretical assignments, to provide extra motivation or as a means to introduce competition among students. Projects here can be part of the assessment scheme, extra-credit or can even replace short internships

    Anyone can work on a project
    Those who succesfully complete a project will receive a certificate
